Our Campaigns

AGWWAS is dedicated to protecting water workers' rights and ensuring that water remains a public good, not a corporate commodity. Our campaigns fight for fair labor conditions, environmental sustainability, and equitable access to clean water for all. Here’s how we advocate for change:

Anti-Privatization

Opposing corporate takeovers of public water services to ensure affordability, accessibility, and quality for all. We believe that water is a basic human right, not a commodity to be exploited for profit.

People Over Profit

Advocating for policies that prioritize communities and public welfare over corporate interests in water management. When profit becomes the driving force in water services, the needs of the people are often ignored.
