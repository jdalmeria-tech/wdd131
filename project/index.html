Who We Are

AGWWAS is a sectoral labor organization representing government workers in the water sector. We advocate for fair labor policies, sustainable water management, and protecting public access to clean and affordable water.

Our commitment is to empower water workers, engage communities, and push for policies that put people before profit. We believe that water is a fundamental right—not a commodity.
